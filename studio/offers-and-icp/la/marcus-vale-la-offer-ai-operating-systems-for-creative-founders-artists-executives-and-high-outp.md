8. Signature method

The public method can be called something like:

The Marcus Vale AI Operating System Method

Core layers:

1. Capture

Collect the client’s live thoughts, voice notes, documents, projects, screenshots, transcripts, and raw material.

2. Corpus

Organize the material into an AI-readable personal or company corpus.

3. Context

Create the durable context layer: who the client is, what they are building, active projects, preferences, constraints, principles, and current priorities.

4. Council

Define how different AI models or assistants are used: strategist, editor, researcher, critic, builder, project manager, memory layer.

5. Cadence

Create the daily or weekly workflow so the system is actually used.

6. Artifacts

Turn raw material into outputs: essays, decks, offers, scripts, SOPs, project plans, products, memos, newsletters, treatments, books, apps.

7. Governance

Install rules so AI supports judgment instead of replacing it.

This is the most important layer. The client does not just get “more AI.” They get better use of their own mind.
