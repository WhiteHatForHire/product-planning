Los Angeles Repositioning Draft

Core pivot

The public-facing center of gravity is moving from New York / institutional / tech-week seriousness to Los Angeles / creative operating systems / embodied AI-native work.

This is not abandoning seriousness. It is relocating seriousness into a better container.

New York remains useful as an arena, signal source, investor/business mirror, and periodic high-density market test. But LA is the more truthful base for the next chapter.

The shift:

Old frame: Symposium Studios, New York, AI-native studio, prototypes, high-end serious business.
 New frame: Marcus Vale, Los Angeles, AI operating systems and applications for creative founders, artists, operators, and high-output people.

Symposium can remain alive, but it should not lead the whole identity right now. Marcus Vale should lead.

Why the pivot makes sense

The New York frame came from Bali and Ubud, where Marcus was craving seriousness, ambition, money, density, and a counterweight to the wellness/expat bubble.

The LA frame came from direct body data.

LA welcomed Marcus as he actually is: long hair, body, board shorts, AI systems brain, music/film/comedy instincts, public witness, walking, buses, beach, Venice, Santa Monica, Muscle Beach, and creative serendipity.

The public positioning should now reflect the lived signal:

Marcus is not trying to become a New York AI consultant. Marcus is becoming a Los Angeles AI-native creative systems architect.
