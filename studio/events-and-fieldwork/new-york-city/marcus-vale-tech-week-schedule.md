NYC Tech Week 2026 — Marcus’s Schedule

June 1–7, 2026 · New York City

Monday, June 1 — Ecosystem Entry

Status access. Don’t over-attend.

4:00 PM — a16z speedrun Pitch Day · SoHo Open RSVP. Observer/operator seat. Hear founder and investor language.

6:30 PM — Official Tech Week Kickoff Party (Gamma x a16z) · Lower East Side Open RSVP. SoHo to LES is 10 min walk. Go straight from speedrun. Work the room 90 min, then leave.

Monday backups (RSVP regardless)

Steal These Workflows: Live AI Demos from NYC’s Top Builders · 6:00 PM · Union Square (Startup Grind)

Agents in Production: The Memory Problem · 6:00 PM · Midtown (Alumni Ventures, SurrealDB)

From Vibe Coding to AI-Driven Development · 4:00 PM · Midtown (Streamlogic) — backup if speedrun is inaccessible

Tuesday, June 2 — Primary Arena Day

Commit to the full day. Don’t snack between events.

9:00 AM — Agentic Commerce: Build Day · SoHo (Microsoft + Tavily AI) Open RSVP. Build day — commit to the morning through noon.

1:00 PM — Ship Your First Agent: a Build Day for CXOs & GTM Leaders · Tribeca (MasteringAI) Open RSVP. SoHo to Tribeca is walkable. Your exact consulting lane.

3:00 PM — Build Agent-First Companies with Airtable · Midtown (Hyperagent) Open RSVP. Subway from Tribeca ~25 min. Leave by 2:30.

5:30 PM — WorkOS + Postman Agentic Night · SoHo Open RSVP. Subway back downtown ~25 min. Leave Midtown by 5:00.

6:00 PM — The Fork (apply for all three, choose on the day)

First choice — speedrun Insiders VIP Dinner· SoHo (a16z speedrun + Replit + Datadog) ⚠️ Application required. Curated. Apply immediately. Replit is in your stack.

If not accepted — How We Build with Agents· Meatpacking District (Betaworks) Open RSVP. Highest thesis-fit room of the week. Default if speedrun VIP doesn’t land.

Backup — MCP in the Wild: Panel & Night of Networking · East Village (M13) Open RSVP. You run MCP. You’re a practitioner, not an observer in this room.

Sober option — Your Lifestyle is Your Longevity @ Maze Sober Social Club · Nomad (IONAVA, Yunara Life, The Maze NYC) Open RSVP. Alcohol-free venue. Anchor-adjacent. Use this if you need a calmer Tuesday night.

Wednesday, June 3 — Thesis Depth

Google AI track in Chelsea. Anthropic is the anchor event of the week.

8:30 AM — Build Your Team for the Agentic Era · Midtown (of All Trades, Genee) Open RSVP. Choose this if you’re starting uptown.

OR — AI Office Hours: Coffee, Claude, and Coworking · SoHo (Tabs + Attivo) Open RSVP. Choose this if you’re starting downtown. Better for real conversations than panels.

10:45 AM — The Architecture of Failure: A 401-Level Masterclass on AI Agents · Chelsea (Google for Startups) Open RSVP. Head to Chelsea by 10:30. Highest technical signal of the Wednesday events.

1:30 PM — Anthropic Founder Salon: Inside the AI-Native Era · SoHo (Anthropic) Open RSVP. This is the highest-signal event of the entire week for you. Chelsea to SoHo is a 15-min walk. Leave the Google event at 1:10.

Afternoon open — use it for follow-up messages from Tuesday conversations.

5:00 PM — The Leverage Live: How to Automate Everything · Flatiron (Leverage) Open RSVP.

5:30 PM — The CTO Mixer: Humanizing Tech · East Village (Devlane, Merge Conflict) Open RSVP. Flatiron to East Village ~10 min walk.

6:00 PM — AI Builders Night · East Village (Braintrust) Open RSVP. Same neighborhood as CTO Mixer. Stay in the area.

Thursday, June 4 — Builder Rooms

SoHo/Chelsea cluster in the afternoon. Do not scatter.

9:30 AM — a16z Speedrun: Startup Culture Brunch for Angels + VCs · Midtown Open RSVP. Then head downtown.

11:00 AM — Founder Lunch Club w/ Fin, Stripe, London & Partners · SoHo (Stripe + Fin) Open RSVP. Subway from Midtown ~25 min. Stay in SoHo for the rest of the day.

2:00 PM — OpenAI Builder Lounge · SoHo (OpenAI) Open RSVP. Lounge format — longer conversations, not a panel.

4:00 PM — Shipping Faster with AI Coding Agents — What’s Working and What’s Not · SoHo (Driver AI) Open RSVP. That title is your consulting pitch verbatim.

5:00 PM — Claude Code Hackathon — Build an Agentic Team · Chelsea (Elevate Online, Imaginary Space) Open RSVP. SoHo to Chelsea ~10 min. Named after your primary tool. This is your room.

6:00 PM — Agents at Scale · SoHo (Modal + Braintrust + Desktree + Tavily) Open RSVP. Tavily is in your stack. Chelsea back to SoHo ~10 min. If Claude Code Hackathon is still running and the room is good, stay — use judgment.

Friday, June 5

Light. No new rooms. Send follow-up messages from Tuesday/Wednesday/Thursday while they’re still warm. Coffee meetings if contacts surface from the week.
