# 5 12 2026 feedback Marcus

> Imported from the source archive and converted to Markdown for searchability. Treat the source path as provenance, not as the current canonical location.
Add anxiety and depression sliders. These are major relapse predictors. Should be in the main check in.

Flesh out predictive functions

“Fortune telling” where you can ask the AI to see where you are in your data cycle to see how high risk you are

The risk factor is important for the user to understand. Most relapses come as a blindsiding thing. Anchor should be able to see it coming based on the data. We need to explore this more.

Having an accountability partner(s) be able to view your data and get a daily report of risk level would be helpful for friends to see if they are at risk for relapse so they can help monitor and intervene. Relapse can be death. This is important. People always self report “I’m fine” but if they check in honestly they won’t be able to hide the truth. The numbers don’t lie.
