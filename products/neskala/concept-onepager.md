# Concept OnePager

> Imported from the source archive and converted to Markdown for searchability. Treat the source path as provenance, not as the current canonical location.
NESKALA

Tell us how you feel.

We'll match you with the right person in Bali.

THE PROBLEM

Open Yelp for massage in Bali. Try to find the one that fits what you actually need today. You can't.

Reviews tell you about the business. They don't tell you whether this person is right for you. So tourists end up at hotel spas, Klook listings, and random WhatsApp referrals — none of which they really trust.

THE INSIGHT

"Booking platforms digitize inventory. Neskala digitizes how a person feels."

Wellness has never had a search engine — it has directories. We're rebuilding wellness discovery around state instead of inventory. The data that accumulates underneath (which states map to which providers map to which outcomes) is the first dataset of its kind.

HOW IT WORKS

1

Scan & feel

Guest scans a QR card at their villa. One open question: "How is your body and mind feeling?"

2

AI matches & explains

Three vetted matches with a two-sentence personalised explanation each. Why this person, for you, today.

3

Confirm & arrive

Local concierge confirms via WhatsApp. Provider arrives at the villa. Cash or QRIS direct.

WHO IT'S FOR

Customers

Tourists, nomads, and residents in Bali who want trusted wellness without scrolling 200 listings. The matching does the work.

Providers

Good but underdistributed local practitioners. Better-fit clients, dead-hour bookings filled, foreign card payments solved (V2+).

Villa partners

One QR card at reception. Better guest experience without managing therapists. Commission per completed booking.

WHY THIS WORKS

WHAT EVERYONE ELSE DOES

WHAT NESKALA DOES

Lists inventory (services, prices, photos)

Sorts by proximity or cheapest first

Treats the customer as a search query

Asks no questions, makes no promises about fit

Race to the bottom on price

No defensible data layer

Listens to state (how you feel today)

Matches by fit (archetype, energy, pressure, language)

Treats the customer as a person who needs the right person

Explains why each match is right for them

Premium customers, premium providers, premium outcomes

Every session improves matching for the next customer

WHERE WE ARE

April 2026 · Pre-launch · Validated wedge: Ubud villa & guesthouse referrals, English-speaking travelers, 24–72 hour booking window. Building V0 now. First 30 days target: 10 vetted providers, 5 villa partners, 10–15 paid sessions.
