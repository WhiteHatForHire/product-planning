# Chapter Generator Later

Do not build this in MVP.

Later, Framewalker can generate new chapters from templates.

The chapter generator should output:

- chapter title
- mission
- visible win conditions
- visible failure conditions
- starting resources
- NPCs
- locations
- inventory
- battle opportunities
- major frame beats
- allowed endings

The generator does not create an unbounded story. It creates the next bounded chapter.

This preserves the feeling of endless adventure without surrendering mission structure.
